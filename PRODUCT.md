# Dispatra

## Product
Dispatra is an on-chain delivery escrow platform on Monad. Senders lock payment for deliveries; riders get paid only when recipients confirm delivery with a PIN on-chain. No middleman holding funds.

## Platform
Web (Astro + React + RainbowKit)

## Audience
- **Senders:** Anyone who needs something delivered - small business owners, individuals sending packages, local merchants
- **Riders:** Delivery drivers and cyclists - primarily in emerging markets (Nigeria, Southeast Asia, Latin America) where crypto adoption is high and traditional delivery infrastructure is fragmented
- **Recipients:** Anyone receiving a package

## Brand / Product Lane
Product (app UI, marketplace, dashboard)

## Voice
Direct, trustworthy, minimal. No marketing fluff. The UI should feel like a tool, not a landing page. Think "Uber for deliveries but on-chain" - functional, fast, no nonsense.

## Colors
- Primary: #22c55e (green - success, delivery confirmed, money flowing)
- Background: #0f0f0f (near-black)
- Surface: #1a1a1a (slightly lighter black for cards/panels)
- Border: #262626 (subtle borders)
- Text: #ededed (off-white, not pure white)
- Muted: #666666 (secondary text)
- Accent: #3b82f6 (blue - links, interactive elements)
- Warning: #f59e0b (amber - pending states, caution)
- Error: #ef4444 (red - failed, cancelled)

## Anti-References
- No purple-to-blue gradients
- No cream/sand/beige backgrounds
- No cards nested inside cards
- No gray text on colored backgrounds
- No bounce/elastic easing
- No identical card grids with icon + heading + text
- No numbered section markers (01 / 02 / 03)
- No tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section
